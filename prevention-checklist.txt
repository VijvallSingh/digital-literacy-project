STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.
- Always double-check before accepting any payment request on UPI apps like Google Pay or PhonePe.
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links received through messages, emails, or social media.
- Avoid installing apps from unknown sources, as they may give attackers access to your phone.
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts and change them regularly.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible for added security.
- Be cautious of calls or messages that create urgency or fear, such as “your account will be blocked.”
- Regularly review your bank transactions and report any suspicious activity immediately.
- Keep your social media accounts private and avoid sharing personal information publicly.
